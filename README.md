# Bennefit – Fitness & Wellness Platform

## Overview
**Bennefit** is a web-based fitness and wellness platform designed to help users improve their health and lifestyle.  
The platform connects users with professional trainers, doctors, and various fitness programs while also providing health tools such as BMI and diabetes tests.

The goal of this project is to build an interactive fitness ecosystem where users can explore workouts, health guidance, and wellness resources in one place.

---

## Features

### Online Trainers
Users can explore professional fitness trainers and view their profiles.  
Each trainer provides mentorship plans with different subscription durations.

### Medical Experts
The platform includes healthcare professionals such as:
- Dieticians
- Physiotherapists
- Doctors

They help users with nutrition advice, recovery plans, and health guidance.

### Fitness Programs
Users can explore multiple fitness programs including:
- Functional Training
- Yoga
- Pilates
- Strength Training
- Nutrition Programs
- Rehabilitation Training
- Dancing Workouts
- Pre and Post Natal Exercise

### Health Tests
The website provides health assessment tools:
- BMI Calculator
- Diabetes Test

These tools help users track their health condition.

### Influencers Section
A section dedicated to popular fitness influencers who inspire users to stay active and healthy.

### Chatbot
An integrated chatbot that helps users interact with the platform and get assistance.

### Mentorship Plans
Users can subscribe to trainer mentorship plans such as:
- 1 Week Free Trial
- 1 Month Plan
- 3 Months Plan
- 6 Months Plan
- 12 Months Plan

### Product Promotion
The platform also showcases fitness-related products such as fitness bands.
